# Ettiquite

1. Take photos and leave only bubbles
2. Don’t shine lights directly at animals when on a night dive. It can disorient and frighten them.

Warnings

1. [Don’t wear a wetsuit that’s too tight or drink too much water](https://www.michaelmcfadyenscuba.info/viewpage.php?page_id=816)
