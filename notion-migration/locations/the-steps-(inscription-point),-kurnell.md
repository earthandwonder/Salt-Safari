## 🤿 Keep reading

You’ll need to pay an entry fee to enter Kurnell National Park. Once you’re in, park at the Inscription Point Car Park and take the stairway down to the cliff face.

Once you reach the bottom, follow the boardwalk to your left until you find steps cut into the rock. Just beyond the steps is your entry and exit point.
